IMAGES FOLDER
=============
Place your real images here when ready:

1. logo.png        — MSF company logo (recommended: 200x60px, PNG with transparent background)
2. about.jpg       — Real photo of MSF team or office (recommended: 900x600px)
3. hero.jpg        — Hero background image (recommended: 1800x1000px)

After adding images, update the src paths in index.html:
- Hero: search for "photo-1541888946425" and replace URL with "images/hero.jpg"
- About: search for "photo-1521791055366" and replace URL with "images/about.jpg"
- Logo: replace the 🛡️ emoji in navbar with <img src="images/logo.png" alt="MSF Logo" />
